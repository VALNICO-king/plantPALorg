Why PlantPal?

We specialize in smart irrigation solutions that take the hassle out of plant care. Our Automatic Watering System uses real-time sensors and microcontrollers to detect when your plants need water and delivers just the right amount—automatically. Designed for home gardens and greenhouses, our system helps you save water, time, and effort while promoting healthy plant growth. We're passionate about combining technology with sustainability to make plant care simple and efficient for everyone.
